// the second brain concept

Clone your brain.
Give it to your AI agent.

Your Second Brain is an Obsidian vault — a folder of markdown files that contains everything you know: your preferences, your principles, your skills, your projects. CLAWG agents read from it at every session. They know who you are, how you work, and what you've built. Your brain becomes their brain.

[ How_It_Works ]

ONE BRAIN.
ALL YOUR AGENTS.

CLAWG agents read from a shared Obsidian vault — plain markdown files you own and edit. Every agent starts with your profile, your preferences, your skills, and your encrypted API keys. The brain syncs between machines via Obsidian Sync, Git, or rsync.

  • [01] Install CLAWG
  • [02] Connect your vault
  • [03] Launch any agent — it already knows you
